About The Data

What's here, and what isn't

Methodology

Predictions are produced by HefML's CatBoost ensemble with a particle-filter overlay for team-strength drift, using SP+ ratings, ppa (predicted points added), talent composite, and a proprietary "stop-the-bleed" index that captures garbage-time performance. Every game carries a projected win probability and a predicted spread; the winner is whichever side holds the advantage.

How to read the numbers

SP+ is a per-play rating, points better or worse than the average team. A spread of +7.5 means the home side is favored by 7.5; negative means the road team is favored. Win Prob shows home-team win probability. Click any team or game for the full dossier.
