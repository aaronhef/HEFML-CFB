The HefML Model how the predictions work

HefML is a stacked three-model ensemble. CFBone (elastic-net), Blue (gradient boosting), and Gryphon (Elo + special teams) each produce a win probability, spread, and total; a meta-model blends them on out-of-fold holdouts and applies a learned home/away bias correction.

Inputs: walk-forward Elo, opponent-adjusted efficiency, QB on/off ratings, transfer-portal net & churn, coaching-cohort signals, rest, travel, talent, returning production (Connelly 2026), and SP+. Spreads carry favorite-size-scaled uncertainty.
